About Me Biography Hi, I'm an 18 years old, beginner programmer who works on a lot of different projects such as the Discord bot "Eutera" and the Unreal Engine 5 plugins "conSole" and "WiMan".
Projects Eutera Eutera was built with the help of NodeJS, with it's first version being released somewhere around late 2020. Eutera (4.5.2) has been released in March 14th 2022 and is the latest version to date.
Projects Decay Decay is an atmospheric in-development game, built on Unreal Engine 5, with a demo version released in late 2019 on Unreal Engine 4. Currently there is no known release date for this project.
